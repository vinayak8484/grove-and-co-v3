We rotate guest roasters every season and train our baristas to do every method justice — espresso, flat white, Aussie-style, filter brews.

Coffee is not your cup of tea? Try our white drinks — chai, matcha, ube — our teas, cold brew, and fresh juices.

    Flat white, cappuccino, latte — our seasonal house espresso from a Berlin roastery.

A warm place, a passionate team

A story rooted
in Berlin.

Grove was born in 2021 when Lena and Marcus returned from two years in Auckland with one obsession — why couldn't Berlin have a brunch spot that felt genuinely alive? Seasonal, unhurried, generous.

Every plate carries that philosophy. Fresh produce from farms within 100km of Berlin, a menu that changes every three months, and a team that actually cares about what they put in front of you.

Come in. Sit down. Let it be a real break.
